On June , the first storm of the season started. It started as a depression and later became a tropical storm. Tropical Storm Arlene passed about  miles from Bermuda. Wind shear caused Arlene to die on June .

Tropical Depression Two started on July . The depression made landfall in Tecolutla, Mexico early the next morning. Two died a few hours after landfall. There were no damage reports from Mexico. 

Hurricane Bret at first, started as Tropical Depression Three on August . It moved north across the Gulf of Mexico, becoming a tropical storm and hurricane as it did so. Bret curved a bit west and made landfall on Padre Island, shortly later it marked landfall on mainland Texas. It died over land on August . Cindy started close to Africa on August . It became a tropical storm on August . The storm quickly became a hurricane before weakening back to a tropical storm. A few days later, it became a hurricane again as conditions got better. At this point Hurricane Cindy reached highest winds of  mph, a category  hurricane. It moved northeast and died on August . On August , Tropical Depression Four started. It soon became a tropical storm and was given the name "Dennis". Tropical Storm Dennis became a hurricane as it moved close to the Bahamas. It moved close to North Carolina and South Carolina. Dennis weakened and moved strangely. It eventually made landfall in North Carolina as a strong tropical storm on September . Dennis died on September  over New York.

Tropical Storm Emily started on August . It head almost due north for much of its life. Wind shear from Hurricane Cindy caused Emily to die on August . 

Tropical Depression Seven just off of Mexico of September . On September , it made landfall in Mexico and died the next day.

Hurricane Floyd was the strongest, costliest, and deadliest hurricane of the  Atlantic hurricane season. Floyd, at first looked as if it were headed for Florida as a category  hurricane. Instead of striking Florida, Floyd struck North Carolina and caused flooding from North Carolina up to some states in New England. Floyd left $. billion in damage from Florida to Maine. Irene formed just north of Honduras on October . It moved north and crossed Cuba as a tropical storm. The next day, as a hurricane, it tracked over the Florida Keys and the most of South Florida. It moved north coming close to North and South Carolina. Hurricane Irene got stronger as it did so. The hurricane moved very fast as it moved away from the United States. Jose started on October  as a tropical depression. It moved northwest and became a tropical storm soon. Not long later it became a hurricane. Hurricane Jose passed over the northern Lesser Antilles as a weak hurricane. The hurricane died by October . As it passed over the islands it left $ million in damage and killed  people. 

Tropical Storm Katrina started on October  as a tropical depression. It became a tropical storm very shortly before landfall in Puerto Cabezas, Nicaragua. Tropical Storm Katrina soon weakened back to a depression. As a depression it made landfall in the Yucatán Peninsula. It died on November , just before it was going to enter the Gulf of Mexico. No one died and there is not much damage.
Hurricane Lenny was a very strong November hurricane. In fact, it was the strongest November hurricane in the Atlantic Ocean. This hurricane tracked east across the Caribbean. Later it tracked northeast, toward the northeastern Caribbean islands. It got stronger very fast, it was just shy of a category  hurricane. On November , it made landfall in Saint Croix.
