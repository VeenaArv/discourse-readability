

Hurricane Kyle ()

Hurricane Kyle was the eleventh tropical storm and sixth hurricane of the  Atlantic hurricane season. As a low pressure area, it moved slowly across Puerto Rico and Hispaniola. The low dumped heavy rains across those islands.

It later dumped heavy rain Puerto Rico. That resulted in six deaths and $ million in damage.

A hurricane watch was put up for Maine on September . This was the first hurricane watch in  years in the state. Elsewhere in New England, the last hurricane watch was  years earlier when Hurricane Edouard brushed eastern Massachusetts.

The storm produced high waves along the coasts of Rhode Island and Connecticut. A newly married couple was killed when high waves knocked the wife into rocky shores. Her husband jumped in to save her. He was overcome by the waves himself.
