

Little Anthony and the Imperials

Little Anthony and the Imperials were an African-American vocal group of the s and s. Their lead singer was "Little Anthony" Gourdine. They began as a group, but changed styles as musical trends changed.

Their hit records included "Tears On My Pillow", "Shimmy, Shimmy, Ko-Ko Bop", "I'm On The Outside (Looking In)", "Goin' Out Of My Head", and "Hurt So Bad". Teddy Randazzo was producer and co-songwriter of several of their hits.

The group faded from popularity during the late s, but continued to record until . Little Anthony still performed the group's old hits for many years, at concerts.
